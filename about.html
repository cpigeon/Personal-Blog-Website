Hi there, I'm Christa!

I was born and raised in the suburbs of Chicago, IL. In August of 2010, I picked up and moved to St. Louis for college. I spent five years at Washington University in St. Louis getting my Bachelor's and Master's degree in Electrical Engineering. In November of 2014, I met the most amazing man, Dylan, who was in his third year of medical school at St. Louis University. We got married in May of 2016 and a week later packed our bags and moved to San Diego for Dylan's intern year in family medicine with the Navy.

Long story short, in May 2018 the Navy moved us to Ridgecrest, CA, a sleepy little town in the middle of the Mojave Desert. We like to say we're in the middle of nowhere but in the middle of everything. We're about an hour south of Mt. Whitney, the highest mountain in the contiguous US. We're about an hour and a half east of Death Valley, which contains the lowest point in the US. We're about two hours south of Bishop, CA, a mecca for rock climbers, fishermen, and hikers. And, we're about two and a half hours south of Mammoth Lakes, CA, which is home to Mammoth Mountain.

After moving, we quickly found ourselves escaping to the Eastern Seirras every moment we could get. Our summers are spent hiking and backpacking under beautiful pines and next to pristine alpine lakes. Our falls and springs are spent rock climbing and fishing in the Owens River Gorge, home to California's most concentrated sport-climbing area. Our winters are spent on the slopes of Mammoth. Our energetic French Pointer, Louis, tags along on all of our adventures (except skiing of course). My hope for this website is to share a tiny slice of the beauty of the Eastern Sierra with you! Enjoy!

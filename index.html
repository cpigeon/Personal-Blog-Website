Hi, I'm Christa!


I was born and raised in the subarbs of Chicago, IL. Long story short, the Navy moved my husband and me to Ridgecrest, CA, a sleepy little town in the Mojave Desert. We like to say Ridgecrest is in the middle of nowhere but the middle of everything. Shortly after we moved and started exploring, my love for the mountains blossomed. We have spent the past three years hiking, fishing, climbing, and skiing through as much of the Eastern Sierra as possible!
